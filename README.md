# Nocturnal Neighbourhood

Nocturnal Neighbourhood provides a platform, for connecting with people
from your college who have the exact skill or tech stack experience that you
have been looking for, through a mobile app. Here people can invite other
students to contribute to their project and they can work together to build
something great! Nocturnal Neighbourhood provides the users with a intuitive
UI and easy navigations and smooth workflow to manage all their projects and
multiple collaborators efficiently, without clutter and with ease.
